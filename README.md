# Kenya Farmers Trade Pattern (K.F.T.P)

**Empowering Kenyan farmers with real-time trade patterns, market insights, and a robust network of service providers.**

K.F.T.P is a comprehensive digital ecosystem designed specifically for the Kenyan agricultural sector. It bridges the gap between information and action, providing farmers with the tools they need to maximize profitability and efficiency during the planting and harvest seasons.

## Core Features

### 1. AI-Driven Market Intelligence
- **Real-Time Prices:** Live-tracking of agricultural commodities (Maize, Beans, Milk, etc.) and fuel prices across all 47 counties.
- **Price Ticker:** A real-time scrolling ticker on the dashboard showing the latest market fluctuations.
- **AI Trend Analysis:** Automated insights into whether market conditions are "High," "Moderate," or "Low" to help farmers decide the best time to sell.

### 2. AgriWise AI Assistant
- **Bilingual Support:** Full conversational capabilities in both English and Kiswahili.
- **Expert Knowledge:** Integrated database on crop diseases (Maize Lethal Necrosis, Leaf Blight, etc.), fertilizer application (NPK/CAN), and yield improvement techniques.
- **Voice-Enabled:** Integrated Text-to-Speech (TTS) for accessibility and ease of use in the field.

### 3. Service Provider Directory & Live Map
- **Verified Network:** Connect with verified providers for ploughing, sowing, transport, and brokerage.
- **Geo-Location:** A live map interface showing the real-time location of service providers across Kenya to reduce logistics costs.

### 4. Financial & Planning Tools
- **Profit Calculator:** A wizard-based tool that calculates estimated revenue vs. costs (seeds, fertilizer, labor) based on acreage.
- **Tender Marketplace:** A secure platform to post buying or selling offers, reaching a wider market instantly.

### 5. Localized Weather Analytics
- **County-Level Forecasts:** Detailed 21-day trends and hourly updates.
- **Agricultural Alerts:** Real-time warnings for heavy rain, extreme heat, or frost tailored to specific farming zones.

---

## Tech Stack
- **Frontend:** Next.js (App Router), React, Tailwind CSS, ShadCN UI.
- **Backend/Database:** Firebase (Auth, Firestore, Hosting).
- **Generative AI:** Google Genkit with Gemini 2.5 Flash.
- **Mapping:** Google Maps Platform integration.
